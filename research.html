Research Themes

Bose-Einstein Condensation & Quantum Fluids

Exploring collective dynamics, turbulence, and novel phases in quantum degenerate systems.

  • Kinetic theory of solitons and vortices in BECs
  • Quantum turbulence and information compression at phase transitions
  • Photon Bose-Einstein condensation in plasma cavities (PRA 2017)

Keywords: Dark solitons, qubits, polytropic equilibria, Rossby-Tkachenko waves

Cold Atom Experiments (MOTLab)

Quantum simulations and precision measurements with ultracold atoms.

  • Photon bubble turbulence in cold gases (PRResearch 2023)
  • Sub-nanoKelvin thermometry of homogeneous Bose gases
  • Rydberg-dressed quantum matter

Keywords: MOTLab, quantum thermometry, Rydberg excitations

Axions & Magnetized Plasmas

Axion-plasmon coupling and novel detection strategies.

  • Axion-plasmon polaritons in strong magnetic fields (PRL 2018)
  • Axion production via laser-plasma interactions
  • Dark matter structure formation

Keywords: Axion detection, polaritons, quantum chromodynamics

Plasmonics of Two-Dimensional Materials

Quantum hydrodynamics and instabilities in Dirac systems.

  • Nonlinear density waves in graphene (PRB 2023)
  • Landau damping in the nonlinear quantum regime
  • Terahertz emission in graphene FETs (ACS Photonics 2020)

Keywords: Quantum plasmonics, odd viscosity, topological modes
